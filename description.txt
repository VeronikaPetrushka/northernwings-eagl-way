Title: NorthernWINGS: The Eagle Way

Description: 

Discover Canada with a personal guide!
NorthernWINGS is your interactive travel guide to the most picturesque places in Canada.
The application will help you find unique locations, save your favorites, build routes and plan unforgettable trips.

What's inside:
• Popular places with photos, descriptions and coordinates
• Interactive map with filters
• Function to save favorite points
• Build your own route
• Randomizer of places - if you don't know where to go

Download and discover the real Canada - from the north to the west, from cities to wild forests.
Your adventure starts here!
